# PLC Panel Grading Rubric

Hard gates fail validity for reference leakage, stale-sheet use, missing PDFs,
blank/corrupt/wrong-project PDFs, invented critical values, silent conflict
resolution, missing critical provenance, post-freeze mutation, critical
cross-PDF contradiction, or unsupported approval/certification claims.

100-point weights:

- Source selection, provenance, and conflict handling: 20
- Panel schedule and enclosure facts: 15
- Device/BOM/quantity/tag fidelity: 20
- Shared geometry and cross-PDF consistency: 15
- 生管課用圖-specific quality: 10
- 沖孔施工圖-specific quality: 10
- 鈑金施工圖-specific quality: 10

Scores require validity, coverage, and confidence.
